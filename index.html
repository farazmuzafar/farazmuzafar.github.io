About Me

I'm Muzafar Soomro, a medical graduate from Chandka Medical College-SMMBMU, Larkana — Class of 2025. Passionate about internal medicine, digital tools, and lifelong learning. This site is my digital space to express thoughts, share ideas, and connect.

My Interests

  • Internal Medicine
    I’m deeply fascinated by how the human body works — especially the diagnosis and treatment of complex medical conditions. My focus is on constantly learning and growing as a future physician.
  • Digital Tools & Design
    I’m passionate about exploring design, productivity tools, and the rise of AI — especially in healthcare. From enhancing patient care through technology to simplifying medical workflows, I believe digital innovation is reshaping the future of medicine, and I love staying connected to this change.
  • Blogging & Self-Reflection
    Blogging gives me a space to reflect on personal growth, mindset, and life lessons. It’s not just an outlet, but a way to build clarity, resilience, and deeper self-awareness. This blog is my journal of becoming — both professionally and personally.
  • Cricket & Babar Azam
    A proud supporter of the Pakistan Cricket Team, and a huge admirer of Babar Azam — his elegant batting style and calm presence on the field make watching cricket even more meaningful to me.
